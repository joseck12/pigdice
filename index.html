LETS PLAY

Instructions

  1. First player throws the dice by pressing the 'Throw dice' button.If
    a.)Any of the two dices stops at 1 they score nothing and its the next player's turn
    b.)Both dices stop at a similar number,the player wins a free throw.
    c.)Dice 1 stops at a number greater than 4,the player wins 2 points to their score.
    d.)Dice 2 stops at a number less than 4,the player loses 2 points.
  2. The first player to reach a total of 100 points wins the game.
